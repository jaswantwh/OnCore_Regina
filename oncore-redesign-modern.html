Clinical Technology

The Diagnostics Behind
Your BioAge Score

OnCore Regina uses the same diagnostic technology trusted by hospitals and research institutions — not consumer-grade fitness trackers.

Gold Standard Diagnostics

InBody 770 Technology

The InBody 770 uses eight-point bioelectrical impedance analysis to produce clinical-grade body composition data. In Regina, we use it to baseline and track every member's skeletal muscle mass, body fat percentage, and cellular hydration status across their longevity program.

Cellular Health Marker

Phase Angle Analysis

Phase Angle is a direct indicator of cellular health and membrane integrity. A declining Phase Angle correlates with accelerated ageing and disease risk. OnCore tracks your Phase Angle over time as a primary biomarker of longevity progress — something no standard gym can offer.

Metabolic Risk Indicator

Visceral Fat Tracking

Visceral fat — stored around your abdominal organs — is a primary driver of insulin resistance, cardiovascular disease, and accelerated biological ageing. Our InBody 770 quantifies visceral fat at every assessment, allowing us to track reductions as your BioAge improves.

AI-Guided Training

EGYM Smart Strength

EGYM's motorised resistance machines automatically adapt to your strength profile, ensuring every repetition produces the optimal stimulus for muscle fibre recruitment. The system tracks force curves in real time and adjusts your program every session — eliminating guesswork from every workout.

Remote Care

Telemedicine Support

Regina members have access to telemedicine consultations with OnCore's clinical advisory network. Whether reviewing BioAge progress, adjusting nutrition protocols, or receiving medical referrals for HRT or TRT — access to clinical support is never more than a video call away.

What is sarcopenia and can it be treated in Regina?
Sarcopenia is age-related muscle loss that begins as early as 35 and accelerates after 60. At OnCore Longevity Regina, we use EGYM AI-guided high-intensity strength training to clinically reverse sarcopenia through precise, measurable muscle stimulation — without hours in the gym. Results are tracked via InBody 770 and guaranteed within 90 days.
Is OnCore a gym or a medical clinic?
OnCore is a clinical longevity centre — not a gym. We are part of a Clinical Referral Network that integrates exercise physiology, nutrition coaching, gold-standard diagnostics, and medical support pathways including HRT, TRT, and telemedicine. The experience and standard of care is medically aligned.
How does OnCore measure biological age in Regina?
We use the InBody 770 — the gold standard in body composition analysis — to measure Phase Angle (cellular health), skeletal muscle mass, visceral fat, and total body hydration. Combined with EGYM's strength and mobility testing, we calculate your unique, data-driven BioAge score at every assessment.

Do you offer telemedicine support for Regina members?
Yes. OnCore provides telemedicine consultation access so Regina members can connect with nutritional coaches, clinical advisors, and medical referral partners remotely — between in-centre sessions or from home.
Is OnCore suitable for adults over 70?
Absolutely. The EGYM robotics were engineered specifically for older adults — motorised resistance adjusts automatically to each user's capacity, eliminating injury risk and ensuring every session is both safe and effective. Our Regina programs serve adults from age 40 through 80+.
